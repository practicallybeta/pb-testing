Imagination Station

A Creative Activity for Ages 7–12

What's This All About?

This activity is designed to spark meaningful conversations and creative thinking between adults and children. Together, you'll explore a thought-provoking prompt, share ideas, and discover new perspectives. There are no wrong answers—just wonderful opportunities to think, create, and connect!

Prompt of the Day

"If you could create a new holiday that everyone in the world would celebrate, what would it be called, and how would people celebrate it?"

Let's Explore Together

Use these questions to guide your conversation and dig deeper into the prompt:

1

What feeling or idea would your holiday celebrate? Why is that important to you?

2

What special foods, songs, or traditions would be part of your holiday?

3

How could people in different countries celebrate this holiday in their own way?

4

If you could design a symbol or mascot for your holiday, what would it look like?

Adult Tips

  • ✦

    Follow their lead: Let the child's ideas guide the conversation. Ask "tell me more" to encourage deeper thinking without steering them toward a "right" answer.

  • ✦

    Share your own ideas: Model creative thinking by sharing your thoughts too—this makes it a collaborative adventure rather than a quiz!
